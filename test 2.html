Overview of an idea

My idea is for a site that would be called the adventures tavern that will make it easier

for role players of any genre and any gaming system the ability to organise game amongst fellow

people in the community as well as share stories of the adventures they might have had with their

party. Done right this could have so much potential maybe not as big as Facebook. It could be used

as an outlet. Or a place for other members to seek advice with story ideas or character designs.

This can also help dungeon masters know the kind of experience level the player may have as well

as see what kind player that person may be, To see if the player would fit with the other members

of the party.




Motivation behind the idea

Just in a site that allows players to play roleplaying games virtually called D20 there is over 5,000,000

as of February 2020 and in one particular gaming system there is about 13.7 million players that play

the game and that number will only grow over time as these kind of games are becoming more popular in society.

I admit it might not be life changing helpful but there is nothing worse than being a new player to the community

and everything being overwhelming and not knowing where to look. This site would eliminate a lot of those issues

and help new people to the community find games a lot easier. The fact that it is a site designed specifically

for role-players should also eliminate the uncomfortableness of being judge as a lot of new players would have this

kind of fear.




Descrition of the idea

The first feature like many of these styles of sites is a secure system so that the user can log in and store all

their information on. The main feature of the site is that the user is able to create a profile with the following information

name, age , experience level , style of player, the area they live in, if they are available for day or night session, which days

they are available , a bit about themselves, if they are a dungeon master or player or both, adding the gaming system they may play

and if they have any interest in playing other system . Which would be available to update based on change of conditions. I would

like to add a spot where the user is able to create profiles for the characters they have created and liked or they regularly use

including pictures they may have of the character as well as stats and spells or equipment the character may have so that the user

has an ease of access to get information on their character in the profile the user has the ability to add and be the only one to

view notes that they mark important during session they may of played . I would also like to implement some sort of rewards system

like a set amount experience for logging in or posting their experience in a session.

There would be access to a chat-based program/messenger that would allow the user to connect to fellow community members or people they have

formed a party with or if a dungeon master needs to contact a player.

There would be a guild-based system (groups) that people can create so for example an artist that may do commission drawings can create a

group to expose their work to the community to gather business as well as a particular gamming system for example pathfinder to allow the

players who are looking to find a game they can post on the guild bulletin board. The guild section of the page will have a feature that

allows a user to view guilds recommended to them based on system’s they may play or if the person already knows what they would like to

search for they can. This will also help new players to find the help they need and be guided into the right direction either by more experienced

user who know that gaming system or the guilds based on what they like. One of the other features would be a video chat with the ability for groups in

scenarios like currently COVID-19 players are still able to gather online so that they can still go on with the campaign as well as have access to

their character all in the one place.

A dungeon master instead of having profile may be able to post small notes of a campaign or recordings of the

session so that the players in the group or anyone who may be interested in listening or reading them. There would be a main bulletin board that

allows players to post their experiences on or ask advice from fellow members for particular ideas also allowing players to add one another so that

they can keep an easy link of contact with them it is as simple as looking at the friends list or in the spirit of the site it being

called their book of contacts.
